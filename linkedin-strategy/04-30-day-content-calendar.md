```
Hot take: serverless isn't just a cost optimization strategy. It's an accessibility strategy.

When you're building for users in regions with unreliable infrastructure, you need:
- Zero cold-start dependencies
- Pay-per-use (because traffic is unpredictable)
- Global edge distribution
- No servers to maintain on the ground

AWS Lambda + API Gateway + DynamoDB + Vercel isn't just a trendy stack. For some use cases, it's the only architecture that makes sense.
```

```
Why PWA? Because many of our users in the Central African Republic don't have access to Google Play or reliable download speeds.
```

### Thursday (Personal Profile)

**Theme:** Lessons Learned

```
3 things I learned building AI products for markets that big tech ignores:

1. Your users are smarter than you think. They just don't have your tools. Design with them, not for them.

2. Connectivity is a feature, not a given. If your product requires stable 4G, you've already excluded half the world.

3. Language is the first barrier. If someone can't use your product in their language, everything else is irrelevant.

Building tech for underserved communities isn't charity. It's the biggest untapped market in technology.

#LessonsLearned #AI #TechForGood #Startup #Innovation
```
