CAREER INSIGHT
From Project Manager to AI Builder
Things nobody tells you when you start building alongside your career.
01
Enterprise skills transfer
Stakeholder management, scoping, prioritization — they matter just as much in a startup of one.
02
The hardest part isn't the code
It's deciding what NOT to build. Scope creep hits harder when you're the PM and the developer.
03
Shipping beats perfection
First deploy had bugs. Users didn't care. They cared that the tool existed at all.
04
Side projects teach faster
6 months building > 3 years enterprise. Not because enterprise is bad — you own every decision.
